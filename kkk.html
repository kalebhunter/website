Who we are

We manufacture replacement parts under the RARE SPARES brand for an incredible range of both new and older model cars. Which predominantly include Holden and ford. We also supply parts for other makes including Toyota, Mitsubishi, Nissan, Datsun, Mercedes Benz, Jaguar and Mini. These parts are available through an extensive network of Distributers and Stockists throughout Australia, New Zealand and South Africa. The huge range of restoration parts and accessories includes; weather seals, rubber kits, rust repair panels, badges, interior components, decals, lamps, lenses, door belts, ute and boot mats, clips and fasteners, hose, glass, pedal pads, lock sets, and much more.
